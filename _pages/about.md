Hi! I am **Junteng Liu**, a first-year Ph.D. candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://github.com/hkust-nlp), advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**, with specific interests in:
- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
